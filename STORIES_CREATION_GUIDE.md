## ğŸ› Troubleshooting

### Camera Not Working
**Problem:** Camera preview is black  
**Solution:**
1. Check camera permission: Settings â†’ LyoApp â†’ Camera
2. Restart app
3. Restart device

**Problem:** Flash not working  
**Solution:**
1. Check flashlight isn't on in Control Center
2. Try toggling flash off/on
3. Some devices don't support flash (iPad, iPod)

### Photo Library Issues
**Problem:** Can't select photos  
**Solution:**
1. Check photos permission: Settings â†’ LyoApp â†’ Photos
2. Select "All Photos" instead of "Selected Photos"

**Problem:** Selected photo doesn't appear  
**Solution:**
1. Wait for iCloud download (if photo in cloud)
2. Try different photo
3. Restart app

### Upload Failures
**Problem:** Story won't post  
**Solution:**
1. Check internet connection
2. Check file size (max 10MB for photos, 50MB for videos)
3. Try again in better signal area
4. Contact support if persists

### Story Not Appearing
**Problem:** Story posted but not visible  
**Solution:**
1. Pull down to refresh story drawer
2. Check if story expired (24 hours)
3. Log out and log back in
4. Story may be processing (wait 30 seconds)

## ğŸ¯ Story Creation Tips

### Lighting
- **Best:** Natural daylight (outdoors or near window)
- **Good:** Well-lit room with ceiling lights
- **Avoid:** Backlighting (subject in shadow)

### Composition
- **Rule of Thirds:** Place subject off-center
- **Leading Lines:** Use lines to guide eye
- **Frame:** Use doorways, windows as natural frames

### Captions
- Keep under 50 characters
- Use emojis sparingly
- Ask questions to drive replies
- Include call-to-action

### Timing
- **Peak Times:** 7-9 AM, 12-1 PM, 7-9 PM
- **Avoid:** Late night (11 PM - 6 AM)
- **Consistency:** Post at same times daily
